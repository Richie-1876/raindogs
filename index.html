About Us

Our goal is simple: for every man who leaves Rain Dogs Society Bespoke Barbers to walk out feeling clean, masculine, and confident.

Everyone is different and not one strand of hair is the same. We believe that the cut and grooming experience should be tailored to the individual. From the initial consultation through to a summary of your cut and how best to style at home, we pride ourselves on providing our clients with a deeply personalised service.

The ethos of our brand is to deliver haircuts that suit the face and style of our clients in a relaxed environment. We are not all about one distinct image; our goal is to make you look good. We appreciate all sorts of hairstyles, however we’re about giving you a versatile look that won’t lose its buzz after it’s washed at home. Each appointment offered is 40-60 minutes long which gives us time to fine-tune your cut and get the best from your visit.

We hope to see you soon!
